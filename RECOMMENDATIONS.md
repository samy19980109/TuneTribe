# TuneTribe Feature Recommendations

A ranked list of suggested features to enhance the TuneTribe platform.

---

## Tier 1: High Impact (Should Build)

### 1. Event Discovery Feed — Rank #1
**Priority:** Critical  
**Effort:** Medium

A personalized home feed that prioritizes events based on user preferences, past RSVPs, and followed organizers. Currently, users must manually filter by city and genre each visit.

**Why:** Increases engagement by surfacing relevant events automatically.

---

### 2. User-to-User Following & Social Features — Rank #2
**Priority:** High  
**Effort:** Medium-High

Allow users to follow each other, view follower/following counts on profiles, and see events attended by people they follow.

**Why:** Builds community and creates network effects — users return to see what people they follow are attending.

---

### 3. In-App Music Preview Playback — Rank #3
**Priority:** High  
**Effort:** Medium

Add a persistent mini-player at the bottom of the screen that can preview tracks from event descriptions, artist pages, or organizer picks using Spotify/Apple preview URLs.

**Why:** Reduces friction — users can hear music without leaving the app.

---

### 4. Event Notifications & Reminders — Rank #4
**Priority:** High  
**Effort:** Low-Medium

Send push/email reminders before events users have RSVP'd to. Allow users to set custom reminder times (1 hour, 1 day, 1 week before).

**Why:** Improves attendance and reduces no-shows.

---

## Tier 2: Medium Impact (Should Consider)

### 5. Event Waitlist — Rank #5
**Priority:** Medium  
**Effort:** Low

When events reach capacity, allow users to join a waitlist. Notify them if spots open up or if the organizer increases capacity.

**Why:** Captures demand and keeps users engaged with full events.

---

### 6. Event Categories & Tags — Rank #6
**Priority:** Medium  
**Effort:** Low

Beyond genres, allow organizers to tag events with attributes like "beginner-friendly," "live DJ," "acoustic," "outdoor," "free," etc.

**Why:** Helps users find events matching their mood and preferences.

---

### 7. Organizer Verification Badges — Rank #7
**Priority:** Medium  
**Effort:** Low

Add verified badges for established venues, record labels, and popular organizers. Display prominently on event cards and profiles.

**Why:** Builds trust and helps users identify quality events.

---

### 8. Event Photos & Recaps — Rank #8
**Priority:** Medium  
**Effort:** Medium

Allow attendees to upload photos from events. Create a gallery view for past events with attendee contributions.

**Why:** Creates social proof and helps users relive experiences.

---

### 9. Direct Messaging Between Users — Rank #9
**Priority:** Medium  
**Effort:** High

Enable users to message each other, particularly useful for coordinating meetups at events.

**Why:** Facilitates real-world connections among attendees.

---

### 10. Event Calendar Integration — Rank #10
**Priority:** Medium  
**Effort:** Low

Add "Add to Calendar" buttons (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook) on event detail pages.

**Why:** Convenience — reduces friction to save events.

---

## Tier 3: Lower Impact (Nice to Have)

### 11. Artist Pages — Rank #11
**Priority:** Low-Medium  
**Effort:** Medium

Create dedicated pages for artists with bio, top tracks, upcoming events featuring that artist, and links to their Spotify/Apple Music profiles.

**Why:** Centralizes artist information and related events.

---

### 12. Playlist Sharing — Rank #12
**Priority:** Low  
**Effort:** Medium

Allow organizers to share Spotify/Apple Music playlists for their events. Display on event detail pages.

**Why:** Enhances the pre-event experience with curated music.

---

### 13. Event Check-In & QR Codes — Rank #13
**Priority:** Low  
**Effort:** Medium

Generate unique QR codes for events. Attendees can check in by scanning at the door.

**Why:** Useful for organizers to track attendance and validate entry.

---

### 14. Dark Mode / Theme Toggle — Rank #14
**Priority:** Low  
**Effort:** Low

While the app is dark-themed, a toggle for light mode could improve accessibility and user preference.

**Why:** Accessibility and user preference.

---

### 15. Multi-Language Support — Rank #15
**Priority:** Low  
**Effort:** High

Add i18n support for multiple languages to expand beyond English-speaking markets.

**Why:** Enables geographic expansion.
